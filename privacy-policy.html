The responsible party for the Ativa Trabalho app respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with **Law No. 41/VIII/2013 of December 23** (General Legal Regime for the Protection of Personal Data of Natural Persons), amended by **Law No. 121/IX/2021 of March 17**, and other applicable legislation in Cabo Verde.

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

The Ativa Trabalho mobile application is a platform exclusively for users in Cabo Verde, initially focused on the island of Santiago with gradual expansion to other islands. It connects unemployed or available workers with entrepreneurs (contractors), facilitating employment opportunities. Workers cannot view each other's data, and entrepreneurs cannot view other entrepreneurs' data. Data access is restricted and requires account creation and login.

The app is free, ad-free, and intended only for users aged 18 and over.

1. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies to the Ativa Trabalho mobile application (available for Android). It covers all users: workers (job candidates) and entrepreneurs (contractors), as well as administrators.

2. Personal Data We Collect

To use the application, you must create an account and log in. We collect the following personal data, provided directly by you during registration or profile update:

  • Full name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Education level
  • Professional skills
  • Island and municipality of residence
  • Open and relevant information for hiring (e.g., additional experience or availability)

Additionally:

  • Login data: Managed by Firebase (Google service), including password recovery via email (no plain-text password storage).

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., racial origin, health data, sexual orientation) unless voluntarily included in the "open information" and relevant for hiring purposes.

Administrators have access to user management tools, but access is limited to what is necessary for system maintenance.

3. Purposes of Processing

We use your data exclusively for:

  • Creating and managing your account (including authentication via Firebase).
  • Facilitating connections between workers and entrepreneurs: allowing entrepreneurs to view worker profiles for hiring purposes.
  • Improving the application (anonymized usage analysis).
  • Managing password recovery via email (handled by Firebase).

Data is not used for commercial purposes, marketing, or any other purpose unrelated to facilitating employment in Cabo Verde.

4. Data Sharing

Your data is shared in a very limited way:

  • Between app users: Entrepreneurs can view workers' data for hiring purposes. Workers and entrepreneurs do not view each other's data outside this context.
  • Service providers: With Firebase (Google) for authentication, storage, and password recovery.
  • Administrators: Restricted access for user management (e.g., technical issue resolution).
  • Authorities: Only when required by law or court order in Cabo Verde.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for non-essential purposes.

5. International Data Transfers

Firebase involves data transfers to servers outside Cabo Verde (e.g., USA). We ensure these transfers comply with Cabo Verdean law requirements through adequacy decisions by the CNPD or standard contractual clauses that provide an equivalent level of protection.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary:

  • While your account is active.
  • Up to 5 years after inactivity or account deletion, for legal or audit purposes.
  • Firebase login data follows Google's policies, but we may request deletion.

After this period, data is anonymized or securely deleted.

7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Role-based access control (workers, entrepreneurs, administrators).

Firebase provides additional security for authentication and storage.

8. Your Rights

As a data subject in Cabo Verde, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Object to processing.
  • Data portability.

To exercise your rights, contact us at ativatrabalhosuporte@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. If necessary, you may also lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority.

10. Minors

The application is intended exclusively for individuals aged 18 and over. We do not intentionally collect data from minors. If we detect data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the app or legislation. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification or email.
